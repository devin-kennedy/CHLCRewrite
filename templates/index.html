Caring Hearts Lending Closet

What we do

We are a medical lending closet providing reusable and occasionally single use equipment to anyone in need regardless of financial status. All of our inventory is donated. We only ask that you pass it on or donate it back to us to continue to help others. Hours are by appointment and are typically Wednesday 10-11am and Sunday 9:30-11 am. Please complete the form to request equipment or to make a donation appointment. We will follow up within 48 hours.

Caring Hearts lending Closet is an approved non-profit in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. Our fiscal sponsor is Transition Town Media and through them we are able to provide donation receipt for tax purposes. If you are interested in receiving a donation receipt, please email us at CHLendingCloset@gmail.com.
